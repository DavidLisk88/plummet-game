Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Plummet ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes but is not limited to:

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store game progress, preferences, and scores locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Database and Online Services

The App may use online databases to support game functionality such as leaderboards, word lists, or game state synchronization. Any data stored in these databases is non-personal and cannot be used to identify you. We do not associate database records with any personal identity.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
