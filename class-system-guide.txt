CLASS SYSTEM
============

There are 4 classes based on your skill rating (0-10,000 scale):

  Class       Icon    Rating Needed
  -------     ----    -------------
  Low          🛡️      0 - 1,499
  Medium       ⚔️      1,500 - 4,999
  High         👑      5,000 - 9,999
  Master       💎      10,000

Your skill rating is built from 8 factors — the biggest ones being
grid mastery (playing on smaller grids), time pressure (playing with
shorter timers), and challenge performance. You need at least 50 games
played for your full rating to kick in.


BEST GAME MODES FOR SKILL POINTS (most to least)
=================================================

Most skill points (fastest way to climb):

  1. Speed Round — highest pressure, biggest XP multiplier (1.75x)
  2. Target Word — focused skill, 1.5x XP
  3. Timed mode with short timer (1 min) — up to 1.6x XP
  4. Hard difficulty on regular games — doubles XP
  5. Small grids (3x3, 4x4) — up to 2x XP since they're harder

  Note: Challenges don't have difficulty levels. Hard mode only
  applies to regular (sandbox/timed) games.

Least skill points:

  1. Sandbox mode — only earns 75% XP (and score is already reduced to 25%)
  2. Large grids (7x7, 8x8) — less XP per game since they're easier
  3. Normal difficulty with long/no timer — base rate, no multipliers

TL;DR: Play Speed Round on a small grid for maximum gains.
For regular games, play Hard mode + Timed with a short timer.
Avoid Sandbox on a big grid if you're trying to rank up.
