Account Deletion

You can delete your Plummet account and all associated data directly within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Plummet app
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to your profile page
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your email address and authentication credentials
  • All player profiles and game progress
  • Scores and leaderboard entries
  • Any cloud-synced data associated with your account

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
